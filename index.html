08Career stops · NJ → NYC

Operator's instincts, strategist's ambition.

I'm 24, based in New York, and I've spent every year since high school inside supply chains from a different altitude — founder, floor associate, co-op, intern at a Big 4, and now consultant on enterprise transformations. The pattern: I learn a level, then move up one, and keep what the floor taught me.

My generation is entering operations just as AI forces the biggest rethink in thirty years. I think that's the opportunity of a career: the instinct to optimize what exists is being replaced by the question of whether it should exist at all — and I'd rather be one of the people asking it than one of the people defending the old answer.
